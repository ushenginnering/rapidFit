# RapidFit Gym Management System

RapidFit is a modern Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed to simplify and automate the daily operations of gyms and fitness centers.

The platform enables gym owners and staff to manage:

- Members and memberships
- Membership plans and renewals
- Check-ins and attendance
- Corporate accounts
- Payments and subscriptions
- Expenses and financial tracking
- Reports and analytics
- Staff and role management

The system features real-time membership validation, allowing receptionists to instantly verify whether a member's subscription is **Active**, **Expiring Soon**, or **Expired** during check-in.

RapidFit also includes:

- Online payment integration (Paystack/Flutterwave)
- Automated SMS and email reminders
- Financial dashboards and reporting
- Installable PWA experience
- Responsive design for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

Built with a premium, minimalistic, and glassmorphic interface, RapidFit provides gyms with an affordable and powerful digital platform that improves operational efficiency, increases member retention, and supports business growth.

## Vision

To become the all-in-one digital operating system for modern gyms and fitness centers.

- Database: MySQL
- Authentication: JWT
- Payments: Paystack & Flutterwave
- Notifications: SMS & Email APIs
